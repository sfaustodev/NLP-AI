1. Service definition

Vox Probabilis is a free research tool that analyzes the spectral characteristics of voice samples and reports four features and a quadrant label, based on methods described in doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19396809.

2. What this service is NOT

  • A lie detector
  • A polygraph
  • A medical device
  • A forensic device
  • A diagnostic tool of any kind

The service measures spectral properties of an audio signal. It does not determine truth, deception, intent, sincerity, or mental state. The mapping between spectral patterns and human behavior is hypothesized and was validated on three (3) recordings from one (1) speaker. Generalization is not proven.
